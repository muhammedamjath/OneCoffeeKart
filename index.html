Our Wayanad Coffee Heritage

Misty morning at our Wayanad plantation

Wayanad, a lush district in Kerala, India, is known for its high-quality coffee, particularly Arabica and Robusta varieties. Grown in the cool climate of the Western Ghats, Wayanad coffee offers a full-bodied flavor with chocolate and nutty undertones. Many plantations use shade-grown and organic methods, promoting biodiversity. This sustainable approach gives Wayanad coffee a unique character that appeals to local and global markets, with plantation tours allowing visitors to experience the region's rich coffee culture firsthand.

Why Wayanad Coffee is Special:

  • Ideal Climate and Altitude
  • Organic and Sustainable Practices
  • Biodiversity
  • Artisanal Processing
