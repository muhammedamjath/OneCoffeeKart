The Journey of Our Coffee

The Rich History of Coffee

Coffee's journey began in Ethiopia's ancient coffee forests, where legend tells of a goat herder named Kaldi who first discovered coffee's energizing effects. By the 15th century, coffee was being grown in Yemen's Sufi monasteries, spreading across the Middle East, Europe, and eventually worldwide.

Today, coffee is the world's second most traded commodity, supporting millions of farmers and bringing people together across cultures.
